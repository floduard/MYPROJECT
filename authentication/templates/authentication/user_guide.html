📘 User Guide

1. Registration

Click on Register from the login page. Provide your username, email, password, and complete the CAPTCHA. You'll receive an OTP for verification.

2. Login

Enter your username and password, solve the CAPTCHA, and verify with OTP if MFA is enabled.

3. OTP Verification

After login, input the 6-digit OTP sent via your selected method (Email, SMS, or Authenticator App). You may choose to remember your device.

4. Profile Management

In the Profile tab, you can change your username, email, or password.

5. Managing MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication)

🛡️ Enable MFA

  1. Go to the Settings page.
  2. Select your preferred method under “Select MFA Method”:
    • Email: OTP is sent to your email.
    • SMS: OTP is sent to your phone number.
    • TOTP: Use Google Authenticator or similar app.
  3. Click “Enable MFA”.

🚫 Disable MFA

If MFA is enabled and you want to disable it:

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Click the red “Disable MFA” button.

6. Update Phone Number

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Find the “Update Phone Number” section.
  3. Enter your new number and click “Update Phone Number”.

Ensure your number is valid to receive OTPs via SMS.

7. Update and Verify Email

✉️ Update Email

  1. Go to Settings.
  2. Find the “Email Management” section.
  3. Enter your new email address and click “Update Email”.

✅ Verify Email

  1. Click the “Verify Email” button.
  2. Check your inbox for a verification message.
  3. Click the verification link to complete the process.

8. Security Features

  • MFA: Adds an extra layer of protection with OTPs.
  • CAPTCHA: Prevents bot logins during registration/login.
  • Email & SMS Verification: Confirms authenticity of your contact details.
